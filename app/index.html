About

The Kabab Shoppe offers a variety of kababs from different parts of South Asia and Middle East. You will find a delicious array of slowly cooked rotisserie meat and grilled meat. Kababs are served with rice, fresh salad or naan. Check out our menu for more details.

All our meats are 100% certified halal.
